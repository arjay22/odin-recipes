Avocado Toast


Description

Avocado toast is not just for hipsters. It's seriously delicious and quick to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado
  • 1 tsp fresh lemon juice
  • ¼ tsp Maldon salt flakes
  • ¼ tsp fresh ground black pepper
  • 2 slices whole grain bread, toasted
  • 1 tsp extra-virgin olive oil, optional
  • ½ tsp red pepper flakes

Directions

  1. In a small bowl, combine avocado, lemon juice, salt, and black pepper. Mash with a fork.
  2. Top toasted bread with mashed avocado mixture. Add olive oil and pepper flakes.
