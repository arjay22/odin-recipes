Home Fries

Description

Pan-fried potatoes are a classic. Simple and easy.

Ingredients

  • 3 medium russet potatoes
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Cut potato into cubes
  2. Rinse cubed potatoes in cold water and drain well
  3. Melt butter in appropriately-sized skillet over medium heat
  4. Dump potatoes in skillet, and stir to coat with butter. Add salt & pepper
  5. Cover with lid and cook for 10 minutes
  6. Cook for 10 more minutes uncovered, turning often until crisp on all sides
